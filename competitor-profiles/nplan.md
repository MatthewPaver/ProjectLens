## Positioning and product

nPlan positions itself as AI-led project forecasting and risk management. Its public product page describes single-project, portfolio, decision-intelligence and schedule-integrity products. It says its models use more than 750,000 historical programmes to forecast activity uncertainty, identify risks and compare mitigations.

## Strength

The strongest differentiator is proprietary schedule history and activity-level machine-learning capability. This is materially deeper than annual public portfolio data.

## Opportunity for ProjectLens

Do not compete on forecast sophistication. ProjectLens can be openly inspectable, public-data-native and focused on year-on-year accountability and evidence retrieval.
