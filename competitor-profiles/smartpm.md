## Positioning and product

SmartPM positions itself as automated project controls for construction. Its public material covers schedule-quality checks, progress monitoring, delay and change analysis, predictive completion, what-if modelling and executive reporting from CPM schedule updates.

## Strength

It turns native construction schedules into repeatable diagnostics and management reports, with detailed schedule-specific metrics.
