## Positioning and product

InEight positions itself as an integrated construction project-management platform connecting scope, cost, schedule, change, field execution and reporting. InEight Schedule also describes using a historical knowledge library to suggest plans and risks for similar projects.

## Strength

Its breadth and connected enterprise data make it suitable for operational project controls inside delivery organisations.

## Opportunity for ProjectLens

ProjectLens should remain narrow. Its advantage is fast public portfolio investigation and auditable evidence, not enterprise workflow coverage.
