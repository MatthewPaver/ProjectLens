# Northstar schedule basis

The accepted control schedule uses a five-day, eight-hour design and construction calendar. Commissioning uses a separately approved six-day calendar.

Ready for service is a contractual milestone. It requires completion of protection testing, system commissioning and operational acceptance evidence.

The baseline assumes planning consent by June 2026, switchgear factory acceptance by December 2026 and no positive relationship lag. Schedule contingency is held before the ready-for-service milestone rather than inside long-duration activities. Available schedule float is owned by the project and changes to that allocation require Project Director approval.
