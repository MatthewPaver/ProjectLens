Evidence boundary

This review compares exported schedule evidence. It is not a forecast and does not establish contractual entitlement, causation or a probability of failure.

Why completeness is explicit

Oracle documents XER as a proprietary exchange format, notes that baseline export is not supported in the standard XER comparison, and states that P6 EPPM XER exports omit risk data. Financial-period exports also contain only periods with values for the project.
